Types of prosthetic solutions

Finding the right technology for you

There are many types of prostheses, from simple mechanical devices to complex high-tech ones that mimic the movement of a natural limb. Here is a quick overview.

⚙️

Mechanical Prostheses

Use hydraulic and other mechanisms to provide reliable support for standing, walking, and daily activities. Strong and durable devices built to withstand significant wear and tear.

🔬

Microprocessor-Controlled

Advanced devices using computerised systems, sensors, and hydraulics to mimic natural gait. They adapt in real-time to different terrains and activities for greater stability and a natural feel.

The fitting process

Your journey to a new prosthesis

Every prosthesis is custom-built for the individual. Our certified prosthetists guide you through every step of the journey — from first assessment to long-term care.

1

Initial Assessment

Meet your prosthetist. We evaluate your limb difference, lifestyle goals, activity level, and medical history.

2

Component Selection

Together with your care team we identify the ideal components — feet, knees, sockets — for your life.

3

Custom Socket Build

We custom-build and fit a socket that connects perfectly to your residual limb for comfort and safety.

4

Gait Training

Work with our physiotherapy partners to learn how to use your new prosthesis with confidence and skill.

5

Ongoing Support

Regular check-ins, adjustments, and maintenance keep your prosthesis performing at its best for years.
